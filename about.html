About

Jordan Joe Cooper

I'm currently Head of Engineering at Zumi, where I lead the technical direction and development of innovative solutions. Previously, I served as Tech Lead and Engineering Manager for multiple teams at Moneyhub, where I honed my skills in team leadership and technical architecture.

My passion lies in leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and drive innovation. I believe in the power of well-crafted software to make a meaningful impact. Through my work, I strive to build systems that are not just functional, but elegant and maintainable.

Beyond coding, I'm an avid reader with a particular interest in books that challenge conventional wisdom and offer fresh perspectives. From Charlie Munger's practical wisdom to philosophical insights from works like "Tuesdays with Morrie," I believe in continuous learning and personal growth.

I occasionally share my thoughts and experiences through The Diary of Humanity podcast, where I explore various aspects of humanity and personal stories of people around the world across history.
