Star Wars in Terminal

July 11, 2022 by Jordan Joe Cooper
Last updated: December 30, 2024

Today I discovered something amazing. If you type 'telnet towel.blinkenlights.nl' in your terminal, you can watch Star Wars Episode IV in ASCII art. It's a beautiful reminder of the playful side of technology and the creative ways people express themselves through code.

This kind of discovery makes me appreciate the command line even more. While graphical interfaces are more intuitive for most users, there's something special about the terminal. It's direct, efficient, and occasionally surprising in delightful ways like this.

It's also a testament to the enduring spirit of the early internet - when bandwidth was limited, but creativity wasn't. ASCII art might seem primitive now, but it's part of our digital heritage, a reminder of how people pushed the boundaries of what was possible with limited tools.
