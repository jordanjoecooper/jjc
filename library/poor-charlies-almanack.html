Poor Charlie's Almanack

by Charles T. Munger

December 30, 2024
I first stumbled across Charlie Munger in a podcast called Founders made by the wonderfully voracious reader David Senra, it was a long time ago but if my memory is right he was talking about Warren Buffet and as I now know you don't do Warren without also finding Charlie and vice versa. 

At first I listened, then I dove in more, other podcasts, books on Warren and that all thanks to Warrens word on the matter led me to Charlie.

I could not afford the previous iteration of this book but when I heard there was a new addition from the Stripe Press I pre-ordered it was cheap compared to the original and frankly an absolute steal given its treasure like contents.

My notes on this book extend pretty far so this review is one I will add to in future. Unlike Charlie, I have much, much more to add.
