The War of the Worlds

Written in the late 1800s and focused on an invasion of earth from Martians. What is shocking is just how descriptive it is and in true HG Wells fashion the avid imagination of technology that you can really visualize in your mind whilst reading.

Library • Jordan Joe Cooper • December 31, 2024

by HG Wells

Shamefully I had only watched movies and TV shows that imitated and attempted to bring to life the original book. Recently I purchased an eBook version of this and then prompts grabbed myself a collection of HG Wells books from the mid 1900s. 

Writing is hard, writing about things that do not yet exist at a time when most of the modern amenities and luxuries we now enjoy either barely existed or were so rudimentary they were available to very few is astonishing. 

The author has a repetative ability to bring things to life that did not exist in his time but now are common and widely used, simply amazing.
